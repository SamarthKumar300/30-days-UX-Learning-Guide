The Priority Framework

AI will automate the execution of UX work. It cannot automate the thinking behind it. Invest accordingly.

Tier 1

Human-Only Skills

Highest priority · Deepest practice

  • Reading the emotion behind user behavior, not just the click data
  • Knowing which user pain is real vs. stated
  • Contextual judgment in ambiguous, messy, real-world situations
  • Finding the root cause of a problem, not just the surface symptom
  • Making confident decisions when data is incomplete
  • Building trust in real conversations with stakeholders and users
Tier 2

Business-Critical Skills

Second priority

  • Speaking the language of metrics — CR, AOV, Retention, DAU
  • Translating any business input into a user-centered design direction
  • Planning projects of any scale — from 1-day sprint to 3-month roadmap
  • Presenting and defending design decisions to stakeholders
Tier 3

Craft + Packaging Skills

Third priority

  • UX audit execution
  • Case study writing — all 5 formats
  • Portfolio scan-worthiness
  • Verbal case study preparation

AI Usage Guide

AI is a thinking partner, not a thinking replacement.

✅ Use AI for

  • Writing and polishing design rationale after you have decided the direction
  • Generating rough research scripts to refine yourself
  • Checking your work against heuristics after you have formed your own view
  • Pattern research — "what are common patterns for X type of screen?"
  • Writing case study prose once you have the story structure
  • Generating stakeholder presentation scripts
  • Brainstorming second-order ideas after you have your primary idea

❌ Don't use AI for

  • Generating your design ideas before you have formed your own
  • Replacing real user observation or qualitative research
  • Deciding your design direction — AI cannot feel the user's emotion
  • Writing your reflection journal — that defeats its purpose entirely
  • Any exercise where the thinking IS the point of the exercise
"AI is a thinking partner, not a thinking replacement."
